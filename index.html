Welcome to Lumos Industries

Lumos Industries' main mission is to address one of the biggest problems facing the United States, Nuclear Waste Management. We at Lumos care about the overall health and safety of the environment. We intend to take action by addressing the inaccurate description of spent nuclear fuel as waste. When in reality it is an overlooked energy rich resource yet to be utilized. Roughly 5% of the total energy within nuclear fuels are utilized during their time within a nuclear reactor. If you care about the planet, there is no reason we should stop ourselves from utilizing an energy rich resource that is safe, reliable and will be creating energy whether or not we decide to do something with it. It is not just a selling point it is the whole reason we started this group. Through Lumos' innovative technology we can tackle one of the most overlooked problems of the 21st century. Currently, the U.S. has no long term or interim storage plans for what to do with their waste, and as the Department of Energy has mentioned on multiple occasions the government's lack initiative on moving forward with a solution is stagnant. In order to side-step the faults of democratic process we intend on developing this technology, and offering it in a consent-based siting process in order to benefit the world.
